# Product Strategy Skills

7 bundled skills.

## `competitive-landscape`

Analyze competitors, substitutes, conventions, and whitespace to inform strategy and design without copying surface aesthetics.

## `experiment-design`

Design ethical product experiments that test a causal hypothesis with valid metrics, guardrails, assignment, analysis, and stopping rules.

## `jobs-to-be-done`

Frame progress people seek, triggers, forces, desired outcomes, and competing solutions without reducing users to demographics or features.

## `journey-service-blueprint`

Map the end-to-end user journey and backstage service system across time, channels, actors, evidence, handoffs, failures, and opportunities.

## `outcomes-metrics`

Define user-centered success measures, product outcomes, quality and harm guardrails, instrumentation, and decision thresholds.

## `problem-framing`

Turn a feature request or diffuse opportunity into an evidence-aware problem statement, scope, principles, and assumption map.

## `trust-privacy-safety`

Design user agency, privacy, security communication, reversible control, and harm prevention into sensitive or high-consequence experiences.
