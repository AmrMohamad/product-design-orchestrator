# Experience Design Skills

10 bundled skills.

## `ai-experience`

Design AI-assisted and generative product experiences with clear capability, provenance, uncertainty, control, review, recovery, and evaluation.

## `collaboration-permissions`

Design multi-user roles, sharing, permissions, presence, comments, approvals, conflicts, history, and accountability.

## `content-design`

Design interface language, hierarchy, help, errors, confirmations, and content models so people can understand and act.

## `data-dense-interfaces`

Design dashboards, tables, admin, monitoring, and operational tools for scanning, comparison, action, exception handling, and scale.

## `forms-validation`

Design forms, data entry, validation, review, submission, and recovery for clarity, efficiency, accessibility, and data integrity.

## `information-architecture`

Design coherent concepts, taxonomy, navigation, labeling, content models, and retrieval paths based on user mental models and growth needs.

## `interaction-design`

Define how controls, feedback, state transitions, direct manipulation, keyboard/touch input, and recovery behave over time.

## `onboarding`

Design first-use, setup, education, activation, and progressive learning around real user value rather than product tours.

## `search-filtering`

Design search, query, autocomplete, filters, facets, sorting, result interpretation, and no-result recovery for realistic information spaces.

## `user-flows`

Model complete user and system flows with decisions, states, alternate paths, permissions, recovery, and cross-channel handoffs.
