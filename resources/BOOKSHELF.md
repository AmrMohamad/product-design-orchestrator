# Professional bookshelf — concepts to know, not text to copy

Use these as conceptual anchors and consult lawful editions/summaries. This package does not reproduce their protected text.

- Don Norman — *The Design of Everyday Things*: signifiers, mappings, feedback, conceptual models, errors.
- Steve Krug — *Don't Make Me Think*: scanning, clarity, usability testing, removing needless cognitive work.
- Alan Cooper et al. — *About Face*: goal-directed interaction design, posture, behavior and flows.
- Indi Young — *Mental Models*: understanding reasoning and aligning support to it.
- Erika Hall — *Just Enough Research*: decision-focused, proportionate research.
- Steve Portigal — *Interviewing Users*: field interviewing and listening practice.
- Tomer Sharon — *It's Our Research*: organizational research practice.
- Kim Goodwin — *Designing for the Digital Age*: end-to-end product design process.
- Dan Olsen — *The Lean Product Playbook*: outcomes, hypotheses, product-market learning.
- Richard Banfield, Martin Eriksson, Nate Walkingshaw — *Product Leadership*: cross-functional product direction.
- Jeff Gothelf, Josh Seiden — *Lean UX*: assumptions, outcomes, collaborative experimentation.
- Marc Stickdorn et al. — *This Is Service Design Doing*: service journeys, backstage systems, co-creation.
- Abby Covert — *How to Make Sense of Any Mess*: information architecture.
- Sarah Richards — *Content Design*: evidence-led content for user needs.
- Ellen Lupton — *Thinking with Type*: typographic systems and reading.
- Josef Müller-Brockmann — *Grid Systems*: spatial order and composition.
- Robert Bringhurst — *The Elements of Typographic Style*: typographic craft.
- Refactoring UI (Adam Wathan, Steve Schoger): practical hierarchy, spacing, color, depth.
- Alla Kholmatova — *Design Systems*: reusable patterns and shared language.
- Yesenia Perez-Cruz — *Expressive Design Systems*: systems that retain brand expression.
- Sara Wachter-Boettcher, Eric Meyer — *Design for Real Life*: stress cases and humane defaults.
- Kat Holmes — *Mismatch*: inclusive design and exclusion.
- Mike Monteiro — *Ruined by Design*: ethics, responsibility, power, consequences.
- Trine Falbe et al. — *The Ethical Design Handbook*: trust, privacy, humane business choices.
- Aarron Walter — *Designing for Emotion*: personality and emotional layers.
- Stephen Anderson — *Seductive Interaction Design*: motivation and engagement with care.

A book is not a universal rule. Use the concept that fits the decision, evidence, platform, and consequence.
